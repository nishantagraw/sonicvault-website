Distribution Agreement

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

This Distribution Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between SonicVault ("Company", "we", "us", "our") and the individual or entity ("Artist", "you", "your") who submits musical content through the SonicVault platform.

By using SonicVault's services and/or submitting content for distribution, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions outlined in this Agreement.

2. Definitions

  • "Content" — refers to all audio recordings, musical compositions, lyrics, artwork, metadata, and associated materials submitted by the Artist through the SonicVault platform.
  • "Platforms" — refers to digital music services, streaming platforms, download stores, and any other outlets to which SonicVault distributes the Content.
  • "Territory" — refers to worldwide distribution unless otherwise specified by the Artist.
  • "Revenue" — refers to all income generated from the streaming, downloading, licensing, or other monetization of the Content across all Platforms.
  • "Net Revenue" — refers to Revenue after deduction of applicable platform fees and taxes.

3. Grant of Rights

By submitting Content through SonicVault, you grant SonicVault a non-exclusive, worldwide license to:

  1. Distribute, deliver, and make available your Content to Platforms globally.
  2. Reproduce, transmit, and publicly perform the Content as necessary for distribution purposes.
  3. Use your name, likeness, biographical information, and artwork for promotional purposes related to the distribution of your Content.
  4. Collect Revenue on your behalf from Platforms and disburse your share in accordance with the terms herein.
  5. Enroll your Content in content recognition systems (such as YouTube Content ID) to identify and monetize unauthorized uses.

You retain 100% ownership of your masters and publishing at all times. This Agreement does not constitute a transfer of copyright or ownership.

4. Artist Representations & Warranties

By submitting Content, you represent and warrant that:

  1. You are the sole and rightful owner of, or have all necessary rights, licenses, and permissions to submit and distribute the Content.
  2. The Content is original and does not infringe upon the intellectual property rights of any third party, including but not limited to copyrights, trademarks, patents, trade secrets, or rights of publicity/privacy.
  3. You have obtained and will maintain all necessary clearances for any samples, interpolations, covers, or third-party content included in your submissions.
  4. The Content does not contain any material that is defamatory, obscene, illegal, or otherwise objectionable under applicable law.
  5. You are at least 18 years of age or have obtained consent from a parent or legal guardian.
  6. All metadata provided (titles, credits, lyrics, etc.) is accurate and complete.

5. Revenue Share & Payments

Revenue generated from the distribution of your Content shall be shared between the Artist and SonicVault as follows:

  1. Free Plan: Content is hosted on SonicVault only. No distribution to external Platforms; no revenue share applies.
  2. Pro Artist Plan: 85% of Net Revenue to Artist / 15% to SonicVault.
  3. Label Plan: 90% of Net Revenue to Label / 10% to SonicVault.

Payment Schedule:

  • Revenue is calculated on a monthly basis.
  • Payments are disbursed quarterly (every 3 months) via bank transfer to the account designated by the Artist.
  • Minimum payout threshold: ₹100 INR (or equivalent in your local currency).
  • If the balance does not meet the minimum threshold, it rolls over to the next payment cycle.
  • SonicVault is not responsible for delays or fees imposed by your bank or payment provider.

6. Distribution & Delivery

  1. SonicVault will use commercially reasonable efforts to deliver Content to Platforms within 7-21 business days of submission.
  2. SonicVault does not guarantee acceptance of Content by any specific Platform. Each Platform has its own content policies and may reject, delay, or remove Content at its sole discretion.
  3. You may select specific Platforms for distribution or opt for distribution to all available Platforms.
  4. SonicVault will generate ISRC codes and UPC/EAN barcodes for your releases at no additional cost.

7. Content Standards & Removal

SonicVault reserves the right to refuse, remove, or suspend distribution of Content that:

  • Violates any term of this Agreement.
  • Infringes upon third-party intellectual property rights.
  • Contains misleading metadata or fraudulent information.
  • Is the subject of a valid DMCA takedown notice or copyright dispute.
  • Violates applicable laws or Platform-specific guidelines.

You may request removal of your Content at any time through your dashboard or by contacting support. Takedown requests will be processed within 5-10 business days, though some Platforms may take longer to process removals.

10. Term & Termination

  1. This Agreement becomes effective upon your first submission of Content and remains in effect for the duration of your active subscription.
  2. Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.
  3. Upon termination, SonicVault will initiate removal of your Content from all Platforms. You acknowledge that complete removal may take up to 30 days due to Platform processing times.

  3. If negotiation fails, disputes shall be settled by binding arbitration in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, with the seat of arbitration in New Delhi, India.
